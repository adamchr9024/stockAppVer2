Here are some websites and platforms where you can track futures prices and related market data based on a ticker or contract symbol (many allow you to enter ticker codes like ES=F, CL=F, etc.):

📈 Market Data & Futures Tracking (Websites)
  1. TradingView — Futures Quotes & Charts
    • Provides real-time (or delayed) quotes and detailed charts for all major futures markets (stock index futures, commodities, currencies, interest rates, etc.). You can enter futures tickers directly in their search bar.
    👉 https://www.tradingview.com/markets/futures/
  2. Investing.com — Futures Prices & Live Charts
    • Offers a comprehensive futures overview with live charts and price tables for indices, commodities, currencies, and more. You can search by ticker or contract type.
    👉 https://www.investing.com/indices/indices-futures
  3. Barchart — Futures Market Quotes
    • Shows futures prices, performance data, and charts. You can look up individual tickers or browse by sector (e.g., energies, metals).
    👉 https://www.barchart.com/futures
  4. Yahoo Finance — Futures Quotes
    • Includes futures for indices and commodities alongside stocks. You can search futures by ticker (e.g., ES=F for E-Mini S&P 500).
    👉 https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/futures
  5. Markets Insider — Premarket & Futures Data
    • Provides pre-market futures prices for major global indices and key futures contracts.
    👉 https://markets.businessinsider.com/premarket
  6. CME Group Market Data
    • Source direct from the exchange where many futures trade (e.g., E-Mini S&P 500 ES). You can look up live data and quotes (though some require registration/data fees).
    👉 https://www.cmegroup.com/market-data.html 📊 Additional Financial Platforms (with futures support)
  7. Yahoo Finance (general)
    • Free finance portal to track stocks, indices, and futures in one place.
    👉 https://finance.yahoo.com/
  8. TradingView (general)
    • Powerful charting platform where you can search pretty much any ticker including futures.
    👉 https://www.tradingview.com/
🔎
How It Typically Works
On these sites you can usually:
  • Enter a futures ticker (like: ES=F, CL=F, NQ=F, GC=F, etc.) in the search box.
  • View real-time or delayed prices, historical charts, and performance metrics.
  • Compare contract months or different types of futures on the same platform.
